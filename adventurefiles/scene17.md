# The Jail

You and your co-adventurer take door to the left. It leads into another hallway lined by 8 jail cells on one side and 5 on the other side. All the cells are empty, except for one. 

You approach the cell cautiously so not to startle the guy. 

"Hello?" You say quietly, not wanting to give any potential nearby guards a headsup. 

"Huh? Who is there? What day is it?" says the prisoner. 

"Hi, we are are exploring this dungeon and trying to find our way out now. Today is Saturday. How long have you been here?" You ask. 

"Saturday you say? Seems like I've been here a long time. I was locked up in this cell when there was an explosion. Man was that loud and a crazy time." he says. 

"What happened during that explosion?" your co-adventurer inquires. 

"Well the guards were talking in the yonder hallway (he points behind his cell towards the hallway you just came from) and mentioned getting in a shipment of dynamite 🧨. Needless to say, one of the guards was quite eager to try it out. They rushed out of the hallway and not even 20 minutes latter I heard a huge KA-BOOM! The walls shook, dust fell from the ceiling in here and I could hear them yollering. Someone got into a LOT of trouble." he says chuckling. 

Suddenly, you realize you have been talking to long and ask, "any idea on how to get out of here? The way we came in didn't allow us to go back out that way." 

"Oh sure, I'd take the 'new' door they created back by the training room. But you are going to need more dynamite to break out. Make sure you grab the treasure that's in the backroom off of the main hallway. But please, take me with you when you leave!" he says.
