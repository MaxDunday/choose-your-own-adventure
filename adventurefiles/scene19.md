# The Armory

After realizing that the guard breakroom is a deadend, you checkout the room nextdoor, the armory. Inside you find dynamite 🧨and a ring of keys! 

It looks like you have the means to reopen the 'new' door if needed and keys that will hopefully open the prisoner's cell. 

Not seeing anything else you might need, you and your co-adventurer make your way back to the hallway door. After sneaking a quick peak you find the hallway quiet and clear. You make you way back to the jail cells.
