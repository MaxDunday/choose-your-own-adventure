# Freeing the Prisoner

You make your way back to the jail cells quietly and quickly. We are still trying to avoid getting caught. 

You arrive at the prisoner's cell. 

"You're back! Did you find the 'new' exit?" he asks? 

"Yes, we found it AND the treasure. It's time to leave this dungeon before we end up residents too!" you say. 

Now which key is it? The key ring contains 3 keys. The first is large with a a lot of notches and has a clover shape at the end. The second is shorter with 3 notches and bunny ear shapes on the end. The final key is average in length and has only 2 notches and a normal looking end.
